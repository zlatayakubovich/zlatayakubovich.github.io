Who Am I ?

I am a junior at LCC International University majoring in Contemporary Communications. I started drawing from a very young age. So, my parents decided to take me to drawing classes at the age of five. With years coming by, I started expanding my skills.

I enjoy creating art around. I took photography classes at LCC. It was a really interesting experience for me and I would like to keep developing my skills in different spheres.
